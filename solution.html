How does it work?
Get started and earn points

Be rewarded to help the planet. With our app, you will gather point each time you recycle. Connect yourself to a container and get points buy recycling. Use your point to buy coupon from our partners.

Step 1
Create a user

If you are not yet registered in PlanetCare, you have to create an account before you can beneficiate from any rewards. To create an account, you only have to enter your real name and your phone number. You don't even need to add your credit card information as you will gather point and not money with recycling

Step 2
Earn points and unlock achivements

When your phone i connected with our containers, you will start getting points as your recycle. You will first see the amoung in grams raise. Then, after a certain amount og grams thrown, you will start getting point. Our point system is: Plastic - 5points/kilo. Paper - 3points/kilo. glass - 2 points/kilo. Metal - 1point/kilo. Make sure to throw in the correct container as our containers can detect the material you throw. You don't get point if you throw your materials in the wrong container. You can start loosing point if you do too many mistakes.

Step 3
Spend your points and get rewards
