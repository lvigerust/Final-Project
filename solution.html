How Does It Work?
Get Started and Earn Points

Download our app and earn points by recycling your waste. Our application is inspired by the succesfull "pant"-system in Norway. Thanks to it, over 95% of cans and bottles are "panted" and recycled.

With our app PlanetCare, you will be able to spend the points you earn on discounts, giftcards and more!

Scroll down to learn more.

Step 1
Create a User

Begin by downloading PlanetCare from AppStore or Google Play. Now you need to create an account by filling out your personal information, this should only take a few minutes.

When this is done you're good to go! You can now begin recycling and earn those points!

Scroll down to read more about how we calculate your points so you can start spending!

Step 2
Earn Points and Unlock Achivements

Now that you've created an account you can start the fun!

Take your waste to one of our containers, simply scan the QR-code on the respective container you wish to use.
Now empty your recycled trash in the container, choose the material you have deposited and watch them points increase while we count your waste!

This is how we calculate your waste into points:
Plastic - 5 points per kilogram.
Paper - 3 points per kilogram.
Glass - 2 points per kilogram.
Metal - 1 point per kilogram.

Make sure to throw in the correct container as our containers can detect what materials you throw in.

Step 3
Spend Your Points and Get Rewards

You can use your points in two differents ways:

Option 1 - Redeem your points as giftcards, discounts, deals and coupons to use in our partners stores. These are easily accessible in our app and you will recieve your rewards in a matter of seconds.

Option 2 - Donate your points to different types of organizations or institutions that helps people in need. Examples of these types of organizations are Redd Barna, Unicef, local animal shelters, Norsk Sykepleierforbund and much more.

Notice! The amount your points translate into NOK will vary.
