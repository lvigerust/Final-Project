Gather points by recycling!

Help the environment and be rewarded!

With our new app, you can gather points each time you recycle. Simply just connect to the app, locate your nearest container and turn your recycled waste into points. Trade your points into discounts, giftcards and much more.

We want to help you to easily find our containers. You can check the location of all containers in your city. You can also look for the nearest containers to your location.

  • Find the number of containers in your city and their position.
  • Find the nearest containers from your position or from any other position
  • Check how many minutes in takes to go to a container
