About Us
Who We Are as a Company

Our company was founded early 2020 in Oslo, with the focus of trying to make more people recycle their waste. This problem is something that we strongly believe can be fixed, and we work hard creating ideas that makes recycling more appealing to everyone. As a company we strive to create products with great quality and with a purpose.

Our Mission

"We want to make recycling more appealing, rewarding and fun for everyone. We wish to make products that serves a purpose, and has a positive impact on the environment!"

Our Goals

❯ Make more people recycle.

❯ Make recycling a fun task.

❯ Make recycling feel rewarding and important.

Our Accomplishments

❯ Created a camp for discussing innovation surrounding recycling.

❯ Made a more advanced, yet easy-to-use recycling station for public spaces.

Plans for the Future

❯ Get more partners onboard and work together.

❯ Expand to other parts of Norway.

❯ Get more investors so we can grow our company, and improve on our idea.

Making the World Cleaner

Recycling of glass, metal and plastic is the key to a better future. We believe in a system that rewards people by recycling. They do something good for the environment and they get something in return. In 2018, the percentagereturn through the "pant-system" in Norway was approximently 95% for bottles, and 99% for cans ("Panteordning for flasker", 2020). We want to do the same with other materials to increase the percentage of trash recycled, and help reduce the amount of plastic and other materials in the sea and the ocean.
