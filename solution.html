How does it work?
Get started and earn points

Any Norwegian citizen can download our app and earn points for their recycled waste.

Our application is inspired by the succesfull pant system that exists in our country. Thanks to it, over 95% of cans and bottles are returned to be recycled.

With PlanetCare, you will be able to spend the points you earned on discounts, giftcards and more.

Scroll down to learn more.

Step 1
Create a user

If you are not yet registered in PlanetCare, you have to create an account before you can beneficiate from any rewards. To create an account, you only have to enter your real name and your phone number. You don't even need to add your credit card information as you will gather point and not money with recycling

Step 2
Earn points and unlock achivements

Connect your phone to a container with the bar code from your app. Then, you will start getting points as your recycle. You will first see the amoung in grams raises as you put materials in the container. Then, after a certain amount og grams thrown, you will start getting points.

This is how we calculate your waste into points:
Plastic - 5 points per kilogram.
Paper - 3 points per kilogram.
Glass - 2 points per kilogram.
Metal - 1 point per kilogram.

Make sure to throw in the correct container as our containers can detect the material you throw.

You don't get point if you throw your materials in the wrong container. Your points can be deducted or the amount you recieve can be lowered if this happens too many times.

Step 3
Spend your points and get rewards

You can use your points in two differents ways:

Option 1 - Redeem your points as giftcards, discounts, deals and coupons to use in our partners stores. This is easily accessible in the app and you will recieve your rewards in a matter of seconds.

Option 2 - Donate your points to different types of organizations or institutions that helps people in need. Examples of these types of organizations are Redd Barna, Unicef, local animal shelters, Norsk Sykepleierforbund and much more.

Notice! The amount your points translate into NOK will vary.
