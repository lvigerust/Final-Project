How does it work?
Get started and earn points

Every inhabitant in Norway can dowload our app and beneficiate from our point system. Our application is inspired by the succesfull pant system that exists in our country. Thanks to it, over 95% of cans and bottles are returned nowadays to be recycled. With PlanetCare, you will be rewarded with points instead of money. Scroll down to find more information

Step 1
Create a user

If you are not yet registered in PlanetCare, you have to create an account before you can beneficiate from any rewards. To create an account, you only have to enter your real name and your phone number. You don't even need to add your credit card information as you will gather point and not money with recycling

Step 2
Earn points and unlock achivements

Connect your phone to a container with the bar code from your app. Then, you will start getting points as your recycle. You will first see the amoung in grams raises as you put materials in the container. Then, after a certain amount og grams thrown, you will start getting points.
Our point system is:
Plastic - 5points/kilo.
Paper - 3points/kilo.
glass - 2 points/kilo.
Metal - 1point/kilo.
Make sure to throw in the correct container as our containers can detect the material you throw. You don't get point if you throw your materials in the wrong container. You can start loosing point if you do too many mistakes.

Step 3
Spend your points and get rewards

You can use your points in two differents ways:

1 - Use it at one of our partners company to buy different types of products or to get reduction on these products. Check the links in our app to see what you can get with any of our partners companies.

2 - Donate your points to different types of organizations or institutions that helps people in need. Notice: For every 2 points you loose by donating to these affiliated organizations, they get 3 points.
