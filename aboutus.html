About us
Who are we as a company

We are a company that was created in xxxx with the focus of trying to stop waste being thrown around, and not being recycled. This problem is something that we strongly believe can be fixed and we will give everything to at least bring the amount of waste down. As a company we strive to create products with great quality.

Our mission

We want to make the world a cleaner place, and our focus is to reward those that have a focus on keeping the world a cleaner place.

Our goals

- Get more of the population to recycle

- Make recycling a normal everyday activity

- Make recycling feel rewarding and important

Our accomplishments

- Created a camp for discussing innovation around recycling

- Containers that is designated for recycling in public spaces

Plans for the future

- Create easier ways of recycling

- Make recycling a fun experience

- Use recycling in a innovative way to reduce climate change

Making the world cleaner

Recycling of glass, metal and plastic is the key to a better future. We believe in a system that rewards people by recycling. They do something good for the environment and they get something in return. In 2018, the percentage return of bottles and cans that gives back money respectively 95% and 98%. We want to do the same with other materials to increase the percentage of material recycling, and help reduce the amount of plastic and other materials in the sea and the ocean.
