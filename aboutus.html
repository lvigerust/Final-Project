Why our company helps?
Main goal of our company: Encourage people and companies to recycle more. Why?
Norway is one of the countries where the trash/inhabitant is the highest with 748 kilos/year according to the norwegian environment agency while it is around 261 kilos/inhabitant in average in the EU countries.
In 2018, 27 out of 133 tons of glass were not delivered to recycling stations, an equivalent of 20,3%
95 out of 222 tons of plastic were not delivered to recycling stations, an equivalent of 42,8%
175 out of 752 tons of paper were not delivered to recycling stations, an equivalent of 23,3%
- Reduce amount of plast or other trash in sea and ocean is one of or goal.
- We hope our idea can inspire and be developed in others countries as well.
